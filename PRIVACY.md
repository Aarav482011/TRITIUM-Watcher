# Privacy Policy for TRITIUM-Watcher

**TRITIUM-Watcher** is a **professional-grade research tool** that prioritizes your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data.

## 1. Data Collection
- **Local Use**: When used locally, TRITIUM-Watcher stores all data (watchdogs, screenshots, logs) on your local machine. We do not have access to this data.
- **Apify Deployment**: When deployed as an Apify Actor, data is stored in your private Apify Key-Value Store. We do not access or collect this data.

## 2. Third-Party Services
- **Scraping**: The tool scrapes external URLs that you provide. Those websites may collect your IP address and other metadata as part of their standard logging.
- **Playwright**: Uses Chromium to render pages. No data is sent to Microsoft or Google through this tool.

## 3. Data Usage
The tool only uses the data you provide (URLs, keywords) to perform the research tasks you initiate.

## 4. Your Rights
Since the tool is open-source and handles data locally or in your private cloud account, you have full control over your data. You can delete your local `watchdogs.json` or clear your Apify store at any time.

For privacy-related inquiries, contact sparklabs2011@gmail.com.
